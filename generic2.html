About

Chantal (She/Her) is a 4th year English & Professional Writing student at York University, mother of 3 children, and avid storyteller. She has served as a Submission Editor with Existere Journal of Arts & Literature, Volume 41, Issue 1, Fall/Winter 2021, and Volume 41, Issue 2, Spring/Summer 2022. Currently she is a Junior Editor for Poetry at York University's Professional Writing Students' Association online literary magazine, Inventio.

I primarily read poetry and fantasy fiction when I am not reading academic texts. My favourite things to watch are mystery, and usually some sort of procedural detective show. My parallel creative activity, when I cannot think of anything to write, is cooking.
